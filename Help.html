How Can We Help You?

At SuitUp, we're committed to providing you with the best experience possible. Whether you need assistance with sizing, styling, or any other aspect of our suits, we're here to help you look your absolute best.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I determine my correct suit size?

We recommend visiting our store for a professional fitting. If you're shopping online, use our detailed sizing guide available on each product page. You can also contact us for personalized sizing assistance.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all unworn suits with original tags attached. Custom suits have different return conditions - please contact our customer service for details.

Do you offer alterations?

Yes! We provide complimentary basic alterations for suits purchased in our stores. For online purchases, we offer alteration services at a discounted rate through our partner tailors nationwide.

How should I care for my suit?

We recommend dry cleaning only when necessary. Between wears, use a clothes brush and allow your suit to air out. Store on proper hangers to maintain shape. Check our care guide for fabric-specific instructions.
